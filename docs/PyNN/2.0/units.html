Units¶

PyNN does not at present support explicit specification of the units of physical quantities (parameters and initial values). Instead, the following convention is used:

Physical quantity Units
time ms
voltage mV
current nA
conductance µS
capacitance nF
firing rate /s
phase/angle deg
